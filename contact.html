Join Us

University of Washington undergraduate and graduate students who have experience with AI are welcome to join The AI Clinic as members. Members of the clinic can expect to assist clients with their research projects and to assist the clinic with offering office hours. If you're interested in joining the AI Clinic as a student volunteer, please contact Director Bill Howe at billhowe@uw.edu or Robert Wolfe at rwolfe3@uw.edu.

For those who are interested in engaging our services, office hours can be schedule direct via our office hours calendar, or you can submit an inquiry at the clinic services pages:

  • Office Hours
  • Short-Term Consulting
  • Long-Term Consulting

We will respond to your inquiry as soon as possible.
